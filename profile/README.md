# JinRobotics  
**Robotics & Autonomous Systems Research Group**

We are a small robotics research team working on practical, end-to-end robot systems.  
Our research integrates vision-based perception, robot control, autonomous navigation, and physical AI to build intelligent robots that operate reliably in the real world.
